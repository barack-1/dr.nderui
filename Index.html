About Dr. Nderui

A Leader with Integrity

Dedicated educator, experienced leader, champion of teachers' rights

Educational Excellence

Dr. Nderui Ndungu brings over two decades of experience in secondary education, having served as a dedicated teacher, department head, and educational leader across Nairobi's secondary schools.

  • PhD in Educational Leadership

Vision & Manifesto

Building a stronger union that truly serves teachers and transforms education

Transparency & Accountability

Implementing transparent financial management and regular reporting to all KUPPET members on union activities and expenditures.

Better Teacher Welfare

Advocating for improved salaries, benefits, and working conditions for all secondary school teachers in Nairobi.

Establishing continuous professional development programs and career advancement opportunities for teachers.

Unity & Solidarity

Building stronger unity among teachers and fostering collaboration across all secondary schools in Nairobi.

Teacher Protection

Providing legal support and protection for teachers facing workplace challenges and unfair treatment.

Educational Innovation

Promoting innovative teaching methods and supporting teachers in adapting to modern educational technologies.
